ABOUT

Since college, I have been dreaming to pursue a career that has to do with technology and computers. I have chosen BS HRM with regards to my father's wish and as his advice regarding the current job market at that time. And I was able to finish the course with flying colors. The graduation made him so proud; however, despite growing through the career choice my father had given and suggested to me, I still have a hunch within me that this is not what I really have wanted.

Alas, during my latest 4-year career as a General Inventory Manager that handled different multiple hats inside the corporation and despite the workload responsibilities I've had, I was given an opportunity to develop a commitment to a new system that our company would adapt -NetSuite by Oracle.

It was the start of my renewed curiosity and passion that I have once set aside during college and something I had experienced touching during highschool --coding. I reviewed my HTML, CSS, and even rushed towards jumping unto learning JavaScript in order to continue developing the said commitment. It was when another challenge came, the API I was going to handle was something I have never heard --SuiteScript, and it had 1.0 and 2.0 versions.

Lo and behold, after thousands of hours, rummaging my brain and the internet of every questions I had during the development, I was able to solo deliver the needed projects for our team:

  • Arranging the dashboards
  • Scripting functions
  • PDF Files

I learned BFO (Big Faceless Organization), CSS 2.0, JavaScript, and SuiteScript 2.0.

It was when I realized, this is my calling. And now here I am. With Zuitt. Doing whatever I can to ready myself in a change of career. Despite the risks.
